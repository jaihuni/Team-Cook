Boil the water in the pot.
Put 1/2 tea spoon of salt, 1 tea spoon of olive oil in the water.
Put 250g of spaghetti noodle to the pot.
Boil the spaghetti noodle for 8 minutes.
Scoop the spaghetti noodle out, and remove the water.
Chop 1 bunch of broccoli, and blanch with salt water.
Chop garlics, onions.
Chop cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, bacon.
Put a pan on a heat and add 1 tea spoon of olive oil.
Stir fry garlics and onions until onions become translucent.
Stir fry bacon until it shrinks.
Add tomato sauce and stir.
Add cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, and broccoli then stir them.
Stir the spaghetti noodle together.
